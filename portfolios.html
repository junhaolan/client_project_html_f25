Showcase Your Story in Portfolio

Let Your Work Speak for You

A strong portfolio is your opportunity to demonstrate what sets you apart as an information professional. At UMSI, building an effective portfolio allows you to highlight your skills, projects, and growth through real experiences—making you more competitive and confident as you enter internships, job markets, or pursue further study. Whether you’re new to portfolio creation or ready to elevate your existing work, UMSI offers the guidance and tools to help you succeed.

Why Portfolios Matter

  • Showcase Your Best Work: Go beyond your resume by providing tangible examples of your abilities, creativity, and impact.
  • Tell Your Unique Story: A portfolio lets you shape your narrative and emphasize personal projects, leadership, and growth.
  • Stand Out to Employers: Demonstrate your technical, design, and problem-solving skills through real, visible outcomes.
  • Track Your Progress: Curate your academic, extracurricular, and professional projects as you develop new competencies at UMSI.
  • Reflect Your UMSI Experience: Leverage the diversity and depth of your coursework, internships, and collaborations as part of your personal brand.

Continue Your Journey

Your portfolio is a living record of your journey as an information professional. As you evolve at UMSI, continue updating your portfolio with new projects, skills, and experiences. Take time to reflect on your accomplishments, seek feedback from peers and mentors, and look for opportunities to share your work with broader audiences—whether in class, at career fairs, or through online showcases. By consistently nurturing your portfolio, you not only document your achievements, but also demonstrate growth, initiative, and a genuine passion for your field.
